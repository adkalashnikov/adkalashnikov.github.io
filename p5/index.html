WHY SoftDev

Trusted Experts
Our development teams are staffed with the top development talent in Eastern Europe. Eastern Europe is routinely ranked as one of the top locations for engineering talent globally. The region has a long history of cultivating experts through their international conference programs and extensive network of educational conferences. Ukraine graduates 38,000 annually ensuring a growing technology community.
Exceptional Value
Eastern Europe is at the top of Gartner’s rankings for its Price to Quality Ratio. Outsourcing is not just about the cost savings, it is about getting the most for your money. You can find lower rates in other parts of the world, but the skill level of engineers for what you pay is what really creates the value. The tech community in Eastern Europe enables even a mid level engineer to have experience with five or six projects.
Quality and Quantity
SkillValue, which ranks software engineers by skill level, ranks 5 Eastern European countries in their Top 10. Likewise, software developers from Ukraine averaged 98% and 88.7%, respectively, across all HackerRank challenges (interestingly the US only scored 78%). But it isn’t just about quality, you have to be able to staff your projects. Eastern Europe has more than a million people working in IT engineering (Ukraine - 200,000). Compare this to the 900,000 unfilled developer jobs in the US.
